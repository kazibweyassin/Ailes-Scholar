How it works

Simple for scholars.
Simple for donors.

01
📋
Scholars apply
Top African students submit their profile — academic record, program, university acceptance, and funding need. Every application is reviewed by our team.
02
✅
We verify & list
Only the best get listed. We verify admission letters, academic credentials, and funding gaps. Every scholar on the platform is genuine and exceptional.
03
💳
Donors fund directly
Individuals, corporates, and foundations browse scholar profiles and fund anyone they connect with. Full transparency on where every dollar goes.
04
🎓
Scholars fly & study
Once funded, Ailes Travel handles visa and relocation. Donors receive progress updates. The scholar begins their journey — and changes Africa from the outside.

Zero capital required
✓
No lending. No risk.
Unlike ISA financing, we never lend a dollar. We match donors to scholars and take a commission — zero capital at risk, immediate revenue from day one.
Scales with volume
100 scholars raising $15,000 each = $1.5M in donations = $150,000 in commission annually. 500 scholars = $750,000/year. The model compounds fast.
Corporate sponsorship premium
Companies pay extra for featured placements, branded scholar profiles, and ESG impact reports. This adds a high-margin revenue layer on top of commissions.
Ailes Travel upsell
Every fully funded scholar needs a visa and relocation. Ailes Travel handles this — creating a natural second revenue stream from the same customer.
